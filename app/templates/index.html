How it works

The Magnolia loop

Four steps from tee to inbox. Each one taking you closer to understanding your game.

01
Play
Head out and play your round as normal. Magnolia doesn't interrupt your game.
02
Log
Enter each hole on your phone — score, putts, fairway, approach. Thirty seconds per hole.
03
Report
Submit the round. Your full strokes gained report lands in your inbox in seconds.
04
Focus
One clear priority for your next session. Not guesswork — your actual numbers.

Take it on the course

Install Magnolia on your home screen — no app store required. Opens fullscreen, works offline for logging.

iPhone — Safari
  1. Open the site in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow, bottom of screen)
  3. Scroll and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" to confirm
Android — Chrome
  1. Open the site in Chrome
  2. Tap the three-dot menu (top right)
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  4. Tap "Add" to confirm

Once installed, Magnolia appears on your home screen and opens fullscreen — just like a native app.
