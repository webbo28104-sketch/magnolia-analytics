Getting the most out of Magnolia
1
Log every detail, not just the score
Putts, fairway hit, approach distance — each field feeds a different part of the Strokes Gained model. The more you log, the sharper your analysis becomes. It takes about 30 seconds per hole once you're in the rhythm.
2
Use approach distances to find your distance gaps
Most amateurs leak shots in a specific yardage window — often 80–130 yards. Log your approach distance on every par 4 and par 5 and your report will start to show you exactly where that window is for your game.
3
Read the coaching analysis after every round
The AI coaching section at the bottom of your report isn't generic — it's written based on your actual numbers from that round. It tells you what you did well, what to work on, and one or two specific things to take to the range.
4
Watch the dashboard trends after 5+ rounds
Your Strokes Gained averages update after each round. Once you have five or more logged, the trend lines start to show your real game — not just one good or bad day. That's when the picture gets interesting.
